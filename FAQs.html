Pet insurance typically covers unexpected medical expenses such as accidents, illnesses, surgeries, and emergency visits. Some plans may include wellness services like vaccinations and check-ups.

Filing a claim is simple! Log in to your account, upload the necessary medical receipts, and submit your claim online. Our team processes claims quickly, ensuring you get reimbursed without delay.

Unfortunately, pre-existing conditions are generally not covered under most pet insurance policies. However, ongoing coverage for new conditions is included once the policy is active.

The right plan depends on your pet’s breed, age, and medical history. Our Basic Plan is great for essential needs, while the Premium Plan provides comprehensive protection for specialized treatments.
